Refund bot

What is this?

RefundBot is a basic Google Appscript that generated a html email when a specific Google Sheet was updated, and then sent the email to our customer support team.

I created this in response to shortcomings in our refund system at the time: A new provider had enabled refunds for customers through their API, but in some cases the refunds required verification by the provider and a manual refund needed to be actioned by the customer service team.

The provider did not however create a function that notified our team when this was done, and product did not deem this a priority issue. As a result, we came to rely on a shared Google Sheet. In order to improve customer experience and reduce followup queries for the CS team, I created this script.

While a bit of a bodge, it is still used today, over a year later - albeit in a bit of a different guise as a Zapier task (Zap). It's not strictly a bot, but it was a catchy name that stuck

If I were to expand on this, I would investigate leveraging the Helpscout API in order to provide the same functions as the Zap I created. I stopped exploring this since Zapier provides a nice interface that the less technical members of the team feel more confident with altering.
