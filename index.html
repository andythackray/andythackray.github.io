Hi, I'm Andy


For the past two years I've been the advanced support specialist for a fairly well known rail booking platform. This means I've been responsible for a whole bunch of things including writing help documents, training level 1 and 2 support staff , and exploring improvements to our customer service processes (including automation).

In my spare time, I've been teaching myself how to code in Ruby and the odd bit of Python, as well as some front end things (hence the web page). I also love to spend time outdoors, and you can normally find me hiking, climbing, or snowboarding when conditions allow.
